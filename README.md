About@nakimoto

My name is Nanami Kimoto and I am graduating with a BS in Computer Science in May 2022. 

I became a Computer Scientist because I love problem solving and innovation. 
I have always been inspired by technology, science, and engineering. I chose a path that would encompass all three disciplines. 
Throughout my undergraduate degree, I became interested in many sub-fields of computing besides general software engineering such as robotics, machine learning,
cyber security, cloud computing, quantum/super computing, computer vision, and many others.
Most of my undergraduate career has been filled with tutoring introductory computer science and math, participating in research opportunities, 
and strengthening my skills as a software engineer by working as a junior developer and data science fellow.
